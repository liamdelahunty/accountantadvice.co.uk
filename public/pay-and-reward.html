Survey Participation

Croner-i Reward has supplied organisations of all sizes with remuneration statistics and advice for over 45 years and is widely recognised as one of the leading providers of pay and benefits data in the UK.

Through its network of database participants, Croner-i Reward has developed one of the most comprehensive pay databases in the country and provides pay statistics to a variety of UK and international organisations as well as government departments, national and specialist publications and a number of leading professional institutes.

Therefore - survey participation from the business community is essential to maintaining the integrity of our database ensuring it remains as comprehensive and informative as possible, producing a robust and credible outcome every time.
